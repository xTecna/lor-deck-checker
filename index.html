Tournament Rules Checker

  • Cardlock: every deck must have different cards from each other.
  • Regionlock: every deck must have different regions from each other.
  • Riotlock: every deck must have different champions and different combination of regions from each other. Also, it is only allowed at most one deck without champions.
  • Collectionlock: the amount of a card among the decks must be at most 3 (three).
